Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 14, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how 'Solar Manager Watch' collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely.

Information We Collect

  • Login Credentials: We store your login credentials locally on your device using Apple's Keychain. This information is not transmitted to any third-party servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Login Authentication: We use your stored login credentials to authenticate you when you log in to the app.

Data Sharing

  • We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law or to provide essential services.
  • The app uses the Solar Manager API, which is owned and operated by Solar Manager AG. We may share your login credentials with Solar Manager AG to access their services, but we do not share any other personal information with them.

Data Security

* We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Your login credentials are stored securely in Apple's Keychain, which uses advanced encryption techniques to protect sensitive data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal information in your Apple Keychain.
  • Deletion of your personal information in your Apple Keychain.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a revised version on our website or in the app.

Contact Us
